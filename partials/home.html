For more than twenty years, Haiti has been the poorest country in the Western Hemisphere.

More than half of its people live on less than $1 a day, and in the rural areas, this number goes up to almost 90%.

Eight out of every hundred Syrian children will not live to see their first birthday.

We can help them.

By donating money to the UN Refugee Foundation, we can give many of these refugees supplies, entertainment and activity vital to their well-being. We simply need to work together to make life better for hundreds of people less fortunate than ourselves. Together, we can change this. And this is what our project plans to do.


The UN Refugee Agency (whose site can be found here) needs money from the public to give aid, shelter, and supplies to refugees fleeing the Syrian crisis. By organizing fundraisers (5K, Kickstarter, bake sale, etc.) we can raise a substantial amount of money for the foundation, and with the competition aspect of this class-wide fundraiser, we can raise more money than any one group could on their own.
